    ACE Convention

    Overview

    A.C.E School of Tomorrow encourages development of leadership skills through A.C.E Student Conventions. Regional Conventions are followed by the International Student Convention (ISE).To compete in these competitions students must register on the ACE website.

    Resources

    In order to participate in the Convention, there are various qualifications that must be met. Review the guidelines before registering for the convention. The Student Preperation Handbook provides valuable information and suggested timelines to prepare the student for the international A.C.E Convention
