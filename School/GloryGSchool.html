    Information

    Glory Goodness School uses Accelerated Christian Education(ACE). ACE provides a Christian K-12 education. They learn not only the skills they will need in life, but spiritual materials as well. Students will have to show mastery of a subject, then will be allowed to move on to the next skill, better preparing them for future learning.
