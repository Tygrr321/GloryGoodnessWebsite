Donate Via Paypal

Step 1

Click the PayPal Donate Now button above to the left

Step 2

Select Donate with PayPal or Donate with a Debit or Credit Card

Step 3

1. Enter donation amount. 2. Login to PayPal. If you do not have a PayPal account, please register an account first.

Step 4

1. In the Add a Note field, indicate the offering type: General, Building, Mission, or Love 2. Your Name 3. Your offering number 4. Church to offer: Falls Church or Springfield

Step 5

1. Select the checkbox Share your mailing address with the Chinese Christian Church of VA… 2. Press Donate Now to complete the transaction.
