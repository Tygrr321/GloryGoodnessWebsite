    Weekly Message

    Jesus is Present in the Storm of Your Life

    Sometimes in life we feel as though we are all alone in the storm. However, as shown in Matthew 8:34, Jesus can calm any storm.

    Matthew 8:34 says, "Then he got into the boat and his disciples followed him. Suddenly a furious storm came up on the lake, so that the waves swept over the boat. But Jesus was sleeping. The disciples went and work him, saying, "Lord save us! We're going to drown!" He replied, "You of little faith, why are you so afraid? Then he got up and rebuked the winds and the waves, and it was completely calm. Then men were amazed and asked "What kind of main is this? Even the winds and waves obey him"
